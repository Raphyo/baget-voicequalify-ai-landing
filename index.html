Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I set up VoiceQualify AI?

+

Setup takes just 5-10 minutes. Simply forward your business line to your VoiceQualify AI number, configure your qualification questions, and you're ready to go. Our team provides guided onboarding to ensure everything works perfectly.

How natural does the AI voice sound?

+

Our AI uses the latest voice synthesis technology to create incredibly natural-sounding conversations. Most callers can't tell they're speaking with an AI. The voice is warm, professional, and adapts to different conversation contexts.

Can I customize the qualification questions?

+

Absolutely. You can create custom qualification flows tailored to your business needs. Set up different question paths, scoring criteria, and routing rules. Our AI learns from your feedback to continuously improve qualification accuracy.

Which CRMs do you integrate with?

+

We integrate with 50+ CRM platforms including Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive, Zoho, and many others. Lead data flows automatically into your existing system with full contact details, qualification scores, and call recordings.

What happens if the AI can't handle a call?

+

Our AI is designed to handle 95% of qualification calls successfully. In rare cases where the AI encounters an unusual situation, it can seamlessly transfer the call to your team with context about the conversation so far.

Is there a free trial?

+

Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial with no credit card required. You'll get access to all features and can test the AI with real calls to see the quality for yourself. Cancel anytime during the trial with no charges.
